What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

14th june 2024

For content I will be using a low quality picture of my Cat, Pickle as an example :3 Content is what you want to show or display, like images and text.

Padding is green: Padding is the space between content and its border, it adds space inside of the area to keep contents from touching the edges/sides.
Border: The border creates a visable boundry around the content. Which you can style in many different ways.For an example use my content picture, look at the gray border around it
Margin is Orange: Margin is the space outside of the border which creates distance between one content and another on a Webpage/website.
