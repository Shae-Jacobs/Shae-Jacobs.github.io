17th june 2024

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I aspire to enter the coding industry, whether it's front-end, back-end, or full-stack development. My goal is to work in a dynamic field where I can continuously learn and evolve alongside technological advancements.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths lie in my determination and constant eagerness to expand my knowledge and skills. I approach learning with patience, effectively balancing study with other activities, such as gaming. However, I acknowledge several limitations: I tend to procrastinate, often delaying tasks when I lose motivation. This procrastination is sometimes linked to my perfectionism, which drives me to ensure things are as I envision them. While this attention to detail can be a positive, it can also lead me to spend more time working on tasks. If something doesn't meet my standards, I may procrastinate addressing it until it aligns with my expectations.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I aim to enhance multiple areas of my personal development while studying here. Firstly, I want to improve my time management skills to maximise my productivity and efficiency. Secondly, refining my problem-solving skills to align more closely with coding (Thinking like a programmer). Additionally, I would like to transition my follower mentality to develop leadership skills, making me take initiative. Lastly, Building self-confidence by taking on a positive perspective and having some affirmation in my capabilities which will establish a strong sense of belonging and competence.
