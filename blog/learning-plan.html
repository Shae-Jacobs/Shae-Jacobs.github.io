Learning Plan for Bootcamp

5th july 2024

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I aim to enter the coding industry with the goal of leveraging my skills to make a significant impact both within my team and on the tasks at hand

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I've been steadily overcoming my limitations, especially through my determination during foundations. One of my strengths that has emerged is my passion for absorbing knowledge and applying it effectively. However, one of my ongoing challenges remains my lack of self-confidence in my abilities. While I'm actively working on improving this aspect, it's a journey that requires time and effort.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

One of my primary non-technical challenges during bootcamp will undoubtedly be developing a deeper understanding of my peers and finding common ground with them. Being naturally shy, I feel nervous about speaking in front of groups as well.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

To me, support during bootcamp means consistently verbalizing encouragement and backing us throughout our journey. It also means maintaining an understanding mindset if any of us encounter challenges outside of bootcamp. Based on my experience during foundations, I'm confident that support won't be something I need to worry about.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

My expectations for bootcamp include:

  • Seeking help by actively engaging with both peers and facilitators for assistance.
  • Maintaining full focus on tasks and avoiding distractions.
  • Building meaningful relationships with each of my peers.
  • Being approachable and supportive so that my peers feel comfortable coming to me in times of need or stress.
  • Demonstrating strong time management skills to complete tasks on schedule.

Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I aspire to enter the coding industry, whether it's front-end, back-end, or full-stack development. My goal is to work in a dynamic field where I can continuously learn and evolve alongside technological advancements.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths lie in my determination and constant eagerness to expand my knowledge and skills. I approach learning with patience, effectively balancing study with other activities, such as gaming. However, I acknowledge several limitations: I tend to procrastinate, often delaying tasks when I lose motivation. This procrastination is sometimes linked to my perfectionism, which drives me to ensure things are as I envision them. While this attention to detail can be a positive, it can also lead me to spend more time working on tasks. If something doesn't meet my standards, I may procrastinate addressing it until it aligns with my expectations.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I aim to enhance multiple areas of my personal development while studying here. Firstly, I want to improve my time management skills to maximise my productivity and efficiency. Additionally, I would like to transition my follower mentality to develop leadership skills, making me take initiative. Lastly, Building self-confidence by taking on a positive perspective and having some affirmation in my capabilities which will establish a strong sense of belonging and competence.
